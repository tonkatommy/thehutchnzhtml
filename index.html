The Hutch NZ Story

We want to help you build comfy homes for your pets!

Here at The Hutch NZ, we strive to bring you the best ideas and designs for your custom home needs. You may have an odd shape area you need to fit a home in. Perhaps you need a slightly bigger or smaller home for your furry little friend. Maybe you want something for your indoor pet that looks like furniture and isn't such an eye sore. Let us come up with an idea and see if you like it. Come to us with an idea and we will help you build it! We believe that your pets should get the best living arrangements that we can provide.
