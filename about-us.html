All About Us

This is who we are and what we want to do!

Hi! We are Alice and Tom. We are a young couple with a love and passion for animals. We both work full time and have started this small business on the side. We currently have 5 Bunnies. Louie (Mini Lop) Lola (Flemish Giant), Leo (Mini Lop) Lacy (Mini Rex) & Lou (Mini Rex X). All our bunnies live outside in two herds. Louie & Lola are bonded and live together. Leo, Lacy & Lou are bonded and live together. Leo & Lacy are rescue bunnies, where Lacy turned out to be pregnant, which we didn't know about, and had a little girl Lou. Along with the Bunnies we also have 2 Red Eared Slider turtles, Squirt & Franklin. Turtles are amazing to watch! To top it off we have an awesome Staffy Boxer X named "ROFL". He's 7 and thinks he's a lap dog still. Cheeky lil dude!
